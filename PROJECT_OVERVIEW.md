# Odysseus - Project Overview

## A Simple Guide for Mentors & Non-Technical Stakeholders

---

## What is Odysseus?

**Odysseus** is a smart personal assistant app designed specifically for engineering students. Think of it as a combination of:

- A **task manager** that understands your academic workload
- A **project planner** that helps you build real things
- A **journal** that reflects on your thoughts
- A **learning companion** that teaches you something new every day

The twist? It's powered by **AI** that actually understands engineering and speaks like a supportive but honest mentor.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Engineering students face unique challenges:

1. **Information Overload**: Juggling multiple subjects, projects, and deadlines
2. **No Clear Roadmap**: Wanting to build projects but not knowing where to start
3. **Learning in Isolation**: Lack of personalized guidance and mentorship
4. **Scattered Knowledge**: Notes, links, and ideas spread across multiple apps
5. **Burnout**: No time for reflection or personal growth

**Odysseus brings everything into one intelligent system.**

---

## Who Is This For?

**Primary Users**: Engineering students (especially mechanical, but adaptable)

**Ideal User Profile**:
- Ambitious students who want to build real projects
- Self-learners who consume a lot of content (videos, articles, courses)
- People who journal or want to track their personal growth
- Students overwhelmed by academic tasks and deadlines

## Key Features (In Simple Terms)

### 1. Academic Command Center

**What it does**: Manages all your academic tasks - assignments, exams, study sessions.

**The AI magic**: 
- Paste your college emails, and AI automatically extracts important deadlines
- Click "Tutor" on any task to get an instant breakdown of what you need to study
- Add study materials and AI creates summaries, cheat sheets, and flashcards

**Real example**: You paste a professor's email about an exam. AI detects "Thermodynamics Midterm on Dec 5" and offers to add it to your tasks automatically.

---

### 2. Builder Hub (Project Workshop)

**What it does**: Helps you plan and execute engineering projects from idea to completion.

**The AI magic**:
- Describe a project idea, and AI asks clarifying questions
- Get a complete roadmap with phases, materials, and timelines
- AI can critique your project for feasibility (like a senior engineer would)

**Real example**: You type "I want to build a solar-powered phone charger." AI asks about your budget, timeline, and skill level, then generates a step-by-step plan with component lists.

---

### 3. Thought Book (Smart Journal)

**What it does**: A private space to write about your day, stress, wins, or random thoughts.

**The AI magic**:
- After each entry, AI provides a brief reflection
- If you're complaining, it challenges you constructively
- If you're celebrating, it pushes you to aim higher

**Real example**: You write "I'm so stressed about this project deadline." AI responds: "Stress is data. What's the ONE thing blocking you right now? Solve that first."

---

### 4. Second Brain (Knowledge Hub)

**What it does**: Captures and organizes everything you want to learn or remember.

**The AI magic**:
- Paste any link, book title, or idea - AI automatically categorizes it
- Get a personalized "Daily Download" - one new concept to learn each day
- Tracks your knowledge history over time

**Real example**: You paste "https://youtube.com/watch?v=xyz - Great video on CNC machining" and AI files it under "WATCH" with a summary: "Introduction to CNC machining techniques for hobbyists."

---

### 5. AI Chat Companion

**What it does**: A conversational AI that knows your context (tasks, projects, journal).

**The AI magic**:
- Ask anything and get responses tailored to YOUR situation
- It remembers what you're working on and can give relevant advice
- Supports voice input and can read responses aloud

**Real example**: You ask "What should I focus on this week?" and AI responds based on your actual deadlines and current project status.
